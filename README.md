# OptiChain – Inventory and Supply Chain Performance Dashboard
OptiChain is a Power BI-based dashboard solution designed to analyze supply chain performance, monitor key KPIs, and enable data-driven decisions.It helps supply chain managers identify bottlenecks, understand supplier risks, and simulate different business scenarios to improve inventory management and fulfillment rates.

# Key Insights from Dashboard
- High Backorder Impact: Backorders result in significant lost revenue (calculated as backorder units × average selling price), with some suppliers contributing disproportionately.
-Supplier Risk Heatmap: Identified Supplier D as the highest risk — low on-time delivery and high backorder rate, accounting for ~40% of backorder losses.
- Scenario Simulation Impact: Simulated that reducing lead time by 2 days increases fulfillment rate from 70% → 75%, directly improving order efficiency.
- Warehouse Utilization Gauge: Shows current utilization at 34% of capacity → clear gap identified against target (100%) to optimize storage and reduce costs.

- Category-Level Performance: Furniture and Clothing categories together account for ~60% of lost revenue → need for increased safety stock and supplier diversification.

#  Business Impact
- Potential to save $6M+ annually by optimizing supplier SLAs and reducing lead time.
- Automated anomaly handling avoids manual checks, saving 10+ hours of weekly effort.
- Improved decision-making with clear visualizations supports faster corrective action in supply chain management.
