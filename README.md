# OptiChain – Inventory and Supply Chain Performance Dashboard
OptiChain is a Power BI-based dashboard solution designed to analyze supply chain performance, monitor key KPIs, and enable data-driven decisions.It helps supply chain managers identify bottlenecks, understand supplier risks, and simulate different business scenarios to improve inventory management and fulfillment rates.

# Key Insights from Dashboard
High Backorder Impact: Backorders result in significant lost revenue (calculated as backorder units × average selling price), with some suppliers contributing disproportionately.Supplier Risk Heatmap: Identified Supplier D as the highest risk — low on-time delivery and high backorder rate, accounting for ~40% of backorder losses.Scenario Simulation Impact: Simulated that reducing lead time by 2 days increases fulfillment rate from 70% → 75%, directly improving order efficiency.
